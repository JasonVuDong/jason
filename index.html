Something About Me

Hi! My name is Jason Dong. I'm an Informatics Undergraduate specialized in Human-Computer Interaction at University of California, Irvine. User Experience has always been my interest because I think that having a good user experience is crucial for the success of a design. However, many designers overlook this vital aspect of the design because we still see many websites/apps that have bad usability. Being an inspired UX designer, my dream is to learn the skills and knowledge to improve the products that we use.

From my project courses, I have learned to effectively collaborate with other people to solve complex problems. I also enjoy working with others because I learn a lot from them. Currently, my goal is to land an internship in UX Research/Design and become an UX designer after graduation.

Besides school, I love to work out and follow car channels on Youtube. I also enjoy travelling to different places and overcome challenging hiking trails in California. My future goal is to travel around Europe.
